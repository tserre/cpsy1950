## Reflection Paper
**Linsley, Feng & Serre (2025)** — "Better artificial intelligence does not mean better models of biology" — *Trends in Cognitive Sciences*
- The inflection point: beyond ~70% ImageNet accuracy, more accurate models become *worse* brain models
- Divergent visual strategies: modern DNNs rely on textures/backgrounds, not human-diagnostic features
- Harmonization as solution: co-training with human feature importance maps (ClickMe)
